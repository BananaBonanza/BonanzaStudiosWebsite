Current Goals

Last updated 11 April 2025

My current goals for life and this website are best summed up with three main categories:

  • Earth Care
  • People Care
  • Fair Share

If those look familiar, then you are also into the permaculture scene. Although in my work life I may make myself seem less permaculture minded, I am an adamant proponent of living more sustainably on this one planet that we have. Within those three categories I have some personal goals:

  • Earth Care
    • Start a garden and harvest my first crop
    • Build a rain-water collection system
    • Begin building a collection of vegan recipes I actually enjoy
    • Bike to the grocery store and back with a rear-cart full of groceries
  • People Care
    • Get HAM radio certified and join the Amateur Radio Emergency Service (ARES)
    • Do more community outreach through tabletop role playing games
    • Write and/or perform music for those in need of joy in their lives
  • Fair Share
    • Participate in community and union events to affect change in our society.
    • I probably won't blog too much about these things

I'll be updating this list as I complete things as well! The current completed goals are:

  • Build a planter box and plant my first seeds
  • Get the HAM radio handbook and begin studying to take the first exam

Thank you for joining me on these journeys and may there be many many blog posts for you to read!
